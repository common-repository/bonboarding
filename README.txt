Create stunning product tours to enhance user adoption and engagement with Bonboarding.

== Description ==
This plugin links your WordPress site to Bonboarding, a no-code platform designed to accelerate digital adoption by crafting engaging onboarding experiences for your users. Whether it's in the WordPress admin or on public pages, guide your users through your platform effortlessly.

[Live Demo](https://bonboarding.com/demo) - check what you can do with Bonboarding.

With Bonboarding, you can:
- Create personalized onboarding tours without writing a single line of code.
- Design visually captivating pop-ups, tooltips, and interactive guides that blend seamlessly with your site's design.
- Target the right users and deliver the right content at the right moment with powerful targeting rules.
- Analyze user behaviors, identify bottlenecks, and continuously improve the onboarding process with insightful analytics.
- Highlight new features with in-app announcements to keep users updated and engaged.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c-dHK7aiBHE

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `bonboarding` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect to Bonboarding and configure your onboarding settings.

== 3rd Party Service ==
The plugin connects your WordPress site to [Bonboarding](https://bonboarding.com), a no-code platform designed to accelerate digital adoption by crafting engaging onboarding experiences for your users.
Using this plugin will send requests to Bonboarding's servers so it can deliver the product tours to your users.

Learn more about Bonboarding's [terms](https://bonboarding.com/legal/terms) and [privacy policy](https://bonboarding.com/legal/privacy-policy).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I create a new onboarding tour? =
Visit Bonboarding's web app, design your tour, and publish it to your WordPress site through the Bonboarding plugin.

= Can I customize the design of my onboarding tours? =
Yes, Bonboarding provides a variety of customization options to match your site's look and feel without any coding required.
